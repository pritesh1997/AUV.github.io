TIBURON

About Us

    Team Tiburon is a group of dedicated and innovative students, based in NIT Rourkela. Formed in 2014, the team works towards designing and developing an AUV capable of performing a plethora of tasks underwater, all without a human pilot! The interdisciplinary team primarily aspires to contribute to the emerging fields of Marine Robotics and Autonomous Systems. As part of its yearly activities, the team also participates in leading national and international level competitions, aiming to gain exposure and test its vehicle against teams from other institutes. What prominently stands up apart from others is the cost-effectiveness and light weight of our vehicles.

Team History

October, 2014 marked the beginning of Team Tiburon when a group of students from varied streams of engineering like computer science, electrical and mechanical came together to deploy their skills. The team, starting with just a handful of members, thus developed its first vehicle Tiburon 1.0 in January 2015. We were the first NIT to reach the final round of the national competition SAVe 2015, and secured the sixth position. The team moved forward with a more advanced vehicle, Tiburon 2.0, possible by the contributions of our esteemed sponsors and the incessant efforts of the enthusiastic technocrats of our team. This vehicle helped us in marking a golden page in the history of Team Tiburon – third position in SAVe 2016. Our vehicle was recognised as the lightest, smallest and most cost-effective vehicle.
